About Pandit Vighnesh Sharma

With over two decades of dedicated experience, Pandit Vighnesh Sharma is a highly respected and knowledgeable priest in Gokarna. He hails from a family of traditional pandits and is well-versed in Vedic scriptures. He specializes in ancestral rites like Narayan Bali and Tripindi Shraddha, helping families find peace and blessings. His calm demeanor and ability to explain rituals clearly make him a preferred choice for both local devotees and visiting tourists.

Services Offered

Narayan Bali Pooja

A crucial ritual performed for unnatural deaths to help the soul attain peace.

Tripindi Shraddha

For ancestors whose death dates are unknown or whose rites have been missed.

Kala Sarpa Dosha Shanti

A pooja to mitigate the astrological effects of Kala Sarpa Dosha.
